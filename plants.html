Blyxa japonica is an attractive grass-like plant with around 20 cm tall. It is ideal for foreground or even mid-ground in a smaller aquarium. It is also used as a background plant in nano tanks. It has a short stem with long slender leaves that arise from an internal structure of short stem which giving the plant a rosette like appearance.
